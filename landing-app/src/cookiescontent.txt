1. What Are Cookies?
Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website. Cookies are widely used by website owners to make their websites work efficiently, provide a better user experience, and gather analytics information.

First-Party Cookies: Set by Creatordesks directly on your device.
Third-Party Cookies: Set by our partners and service providers (e.g., Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel).
Session Cookies: Temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser.
Persistent Cookies: Remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them.
2. Why We Use Cookies
We use cookies and similar technologies for several purposes:

2.1 Essential Cookies (Strictly Necessary)
These cookies are essential for the Service to function properly. Without these cookies, core features would not work.

Authentication: Keep you logged in to your account and remember your session.
Security: Protect against fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access.
Load Balancing: Distribute traffic across our servers for optimal performance.
User Preferences: Remember your language preference and other settings.
2.2 Analytics Cookies
These cookies help us understand how visitors use our website and Service so we can improve the user experience.

Google Analytics: Collects anonymous usage data, including pages visited, time spent on site, referral sources, and device information.
Usage Patterns: Track which features are most used, identify bugs, and understand user behavior.
Performance Monitoring: Measure page load times and identify technical issues.
2.3 Marketing and Advertising Cookies
These cookies are used to deliver relevant ads and track the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns.

Facebook Pixel: Tracks conversions from Facebook ads, builds custom audiences, and optimizes ad delivery.
Google Ads: Tracks conversions from Google ads and enables remarketing campaigns.
Retargeting: Shows you relevant ads on third-party websites based on your previous visits to our site.
2.4 Functional Cookies
These cookies enhance the functionality of the Service and provide personalized features.

Personalization: Remember your preferences, such as dashboard layout or notification settings.
Feature Enhancements: Enable advanced features like real-time previews and interactive demos.
Content Recommendations: Suggest relevant templates or automation ideas based on your usage.

6. Your Cookie Choices
You have several options to manage and control cookies:

6.1 Browser Settings
Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can:

Block All Cookies: Prevent all cookies from being set (may break website functionality)
Block Third-Party Cookies: Block cookies from external domains while allowing first-party cookies
Delete Cookies: Clear existing cookies from your browser
Receive Alerts: Get notifications when websites attempt to set cookies
Google Chrome: Cookie Settings Guide
Mozilla Firefox: Cookie Settings Guide
Safari: Cookie Settings Guide
Microsoft Edge: Cookie Settings Guide
6.2 Opt-Out Tools
You can opt out of certain tracking technologies:

Google Analytics: Install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on: https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout
Facebook Pixel: Adjust your Facebook Ad Preferences: https://www.facebook.com/settings?tab=ads
Industry Opt-Out Tools: Visit the Digital Advertising Alliance opt-out page: http://optout.aboutads.info/ or the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out page: http://optout.networkadvertising.org/
6.3 Do Not Track (DNT)
Some browsers include a "Do Not Track" (DNT) feature that signals to websites that you do not want to be tracked. However, there is no industry standard for how websites should respond to DNT signals. Creatordesks currently does not respond to DNT signals, but you can use the opt-out methods described above.

6.4 Mobile Device Settings
On mobile devices, you can limit tracking through device settings:

iOS: Settings > Privacy > Tracking > Disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track"
Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization
Reset Advertising ID: Periodically reset your advertising identifier to limit cross-app tracking
7. Impact of Disabling Cookies

Essential cookies are required for core functionality. If you block essential cookies, you will not be able to use the Service.
